Recommendations

Make the most of your trip

Practical travel tips and local favourites — eat well, see something new, swim in a lake.

Travel

Suggested flights

  • Friday: London → Poznań from around £55.
  • Monday: return flights from Poznań from around £65.
  • Berlin has cheaper flights, but you will need a FlixBus transfer and to be based in Berlin.
  • Travelling from Paris? Wrocław Airport tends to be cheaper.
  • Travelling from Milan? Poznań and Wrocław are similarly priced.

Car rental

KaizenRent is available at both Poznań and Wrocław airports — reliable, cheap, and no issues so far.

Longer stay?

Wrocław is well worth a visit in its own right. Kraków and Gdańsk are both around 5 hours away by train or car.
