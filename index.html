Places with character

Church, palace and a very Polish setting

Lusowo sits just outside Poznań, a city known for trade, engineering, solid train connections and a stubborn sense of regional identity. It is one of Poland's oldest urban centres and still manages to feel practical, proud and slightly contrarian.

The church of St Jadwiga and St James the Apostle serves a historic local parish, while Pałac Szczepowice offers the quieter, estate-side mood for the evening celebration.
